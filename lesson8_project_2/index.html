Beyonce the Activist

Beyonce is an intersectional feminist advocating for the Black community, LGBTQ+ community, and women. She weaves the plights of the black community and women into her art. Her latest studio album, Lemonade, is a celebration of black womanhood. It's a love letter to black women, specifically scorned black women. She uses her wealth, as well as influence to positively afffect organizations representing the black community as well as other disenfranchised communtiies.. She has donated siginficant sums of money to organizations such as Chime for Change, HBCUs, the Survivor Foundation,and Black Lives Matter.
